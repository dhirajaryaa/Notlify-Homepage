What platforms does Notlify support?
Notlify supports 50+ platforms including Gmail, GitHub, YouTube, DevTO, Hashnode, Slack, Discord, Twitter, LinkedIn, Figma, and many more. We're constantly adding new integrations based on user feedback.
Is there a free trial available?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to get started. You can cancel anytime during the trial period.
How secure is my data?
We take security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. We're SOC 2 compliant and never store your actual notification content, only metadata.
Can I customize notification filters?
Absolutely! Notlify offers advanced filtering options including keyword filtering, source prioritization, time-based rules, and AI-powered smart filtering that learns from your behavior.
What's the pricing after the trial?
We offer flexible pricing starting at $9/month for individual users and $19/month for teams. Enterprise plans are available for larger organizations. All plans include unlimited notifications and integrations.
